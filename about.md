Hello.

You have happened upon my digital commonplace book. If you are new to the idea of a commonplace book, you can read more about it [here](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Commonplace_book).

What you will see in my commonplaces are passages from books that I am reading, or have read, that I find interesting. I do not focus my reading on any specific subject matter, over the long-term, but I do tend over the short-term to explore specific authors or topics.
