Starting a redesign of foobt.net with the following goals in mind:

1. It will be responsive [[Responsive web design, Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Responsive_web_design)].
2. I will not use PHP (time to learn something new).
3. I will *probably* use a document/NoSQL database such as [MongoDB](https://www.mongodb.com/).

For now I have settled upon the following for my toolkit:

* [Spectre.css](https://picturepan2.github.io/spectre/)

And I am considering the following:

* [ClojureScript](https://github.com/clojure/clojurescript) via [Luminus](http://www.luminusweb.net/)
* [MEAN](http://mean.io/#!/)
* [SANE](http://sanestack.com/)
